Privacy Policy

This policy explains how information related to the AxonASP website pages and downloads is handled. It is intended for developers, teams, and organizations evaluating or using AxonASP resources.

Scope

  • This policy applies to website pages and related distribution resources.
  • It does not replace policies required by your own applications built with AxonASP.

1. Information categories

Technical usage data

Basic technical metadata may be processed to operate and secure site delivery, including request and browser environment details.

Voluntary contact data

If you contact the project maintainers, the information you submit is used only for support, communication, and follow-up about your request.

2. Purpose of processing

Operate the website

Ensure availability, reliability, and security of pages and downloadable resources.

Improve technical documentation

Use aggregated insights to improve content quality, navigation clarity, and developer onboarding paths.

Respond to support requests

Handle project questions and issue reports submitted by users.

3. Data sharing and retention

Data sharing

Information is not sold. Sharing may occur only when operationally necessary, legally required, or requested by the user.

Retention period

Information is retained only for as long as required to meet operational, legal, and security obligations.

4. Your controls

You may request clarification about this policy or ask for updates regarding your submitted information through official project channels.
